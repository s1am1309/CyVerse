My Confession

Mowry, amar moner ekta ekmatro rani tumi. Amar jibone jei shob vul hoye geche, ami tar jonno onek dukkho peyechi. Kintu ekta jinis ami always jani—amar bhalobasha shotti chhilo, chhilo na kono nijer interest er jonno. Tumi jokhoni haso, amar duniya roddur hoye uthe. Tumi jokhoni chole jao, amar prithibi ekdom shunno lage. Ami kokhono perfect chele chilam na, but amar bhalobasha always pure chhilo.

Hoyto tumi amake ar kokhono ager moto bhalobey na, but ami promise kori, ami chesta korbo ekta bhalo manush hoye tumar jonno. Karor shathe na thakte pari, tateo tumi amar moner majhe chirodin thakbe.

Bhalobashi. Ekti shobdo, jeta amar prithibir shobcheye boro satyi. And amar oi satyi'r naam holo — Mowry.
